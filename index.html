Welcome to our Home

A place of love, care, and compassion for the elderly. Founded by the VIDT Foundation in 2021, our mission is to ensure that every elderly person in our care experiences dignity, comfort, and a sense of belonging.

Our Mission

To provide a safe and caring home for elderly individuals, offering 24/7 medical support, emotional counseling, and the assistance they need. We ensure they live their golden years with dignity, love, and comfort.

Get Involved

We believe that every small act of kindness can make a big difference in the lives of our residents. Here are some ways you can get involved:
  • Donate: Support us financially or with essential items.
  • Volunteer: Spend time at our home and interact with the elderly.
  • Sponsor: Become a long-term sponsor and support one or more of our residents.

How You Can Help

There are several ways to make a meaningful impact at Arram Old Age Home:

  1. Celebrate Special Occasions
    Turn your birthdays, anniversaries, or festive days into a memorable experience for our residents by sponsoring a special meal or organizing an event.
  2. In Memory of a Loved One
    Honor the memory of someone dear by sponsoring a day of meals or medical care in their name.
  3. Monthly Donations
    Help meet our monthly needs by contributing groceries, toiletries, medical supplies, or financial aid.
  4. Sponsor Essential Services
    Provide financial support to cover medical expenses, wellness programs, and rehabilitation services.
  5. Volunteer Your Time
    Spend time with our residents, offering companionship and support, and make a difference in their lives.
  6. Support Infrastructure Development
    Help us improve and expand our facilities by donating towards building maintenance, upgrades, or new projects.
